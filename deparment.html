The Department

Training and Placement Cell of Trident Group of Institutions, sincerely believes in comprehensively training the students to face the corporate world and have a successful professional career. The department of this college provides guidance and all the assistance to the students in order to achieve their career goals. The unit takes right steps in identifying the demands of the current industry and prepares the students towards this need. Extra care is taken to design and train the students to make them ready for the industry and develop the students to face the corporate world confidently.
